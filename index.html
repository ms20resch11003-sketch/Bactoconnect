About the Conference

Recognizing the growing research interest in Bacterial Cellulose (BC) across diverse fields such as polymer science, textiles, biomaterials, cosmetics, and environmental engineering, Bactoconnect serves as a premier gathering for experts and enthusiasts alike.

Organized by the Cellulose and Composite Group, IIT Hyderabad.

Message from the Founder

"Welcome to Bactoconnect 2026. The Cellulose and Composite Group at IIT Hyderabad is delighted to announce the second edition of BactoConnect - an annual conference dedicated to fostering global dialogue and collaboration around bacterial cellulose, a remarkable and sustainable biopolymer with transformative potential across industries. This event serves as a platform for leading researchers and industry experts to share insights, discuss breakthroughs, and envision the future of bacterial cellulose in science, technology, and applications. Commemorating the birth anniversary of A.J. Brown, the discoverer of bacterial cellulose, April 27th is celebrated as a special day by our group.."

Prof. Mudrika Khandelwal

Founder, Bactoconnect
Cellulose and Composite Group, IIT Hyderabad
